  • CART

    Communication Access Realtime Translation, also known as CART, transforms spoken information into word-for-word text on your computer screen or smartphone. Using either a specialized shorthand machine or cutting-edge speech recognition technologies our captioners create verbatim text of any event--meetings, workshops, lectures, webinars….You name it. Customers who want to know every word spoken with close-to-exact accuracy ask for CART services. The transcripts are free. And exceedingly useful.

  • TypeWell & C-Print

    For a condensed, easy-to-read option many of our customers request TypeWell or C-Print. These technologies rely on transcribers using abbreviation software that allows them to capture the essence of what’s said in real time. This process eliminates redundancy and erroneous words--you read what the speaker means. TypeWell and C-Print are often used in classrooms as well as an increasing variety of business settings and webcasts. If you’ve never experienced this method of speech-to-text, give it a shot. Contact us for a free demo. Today!

  • Media Captioning

    CaptionAccess will add text to your videos and audio clips. Do you have media on your website that isn’t accessible to deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals? Almost all sites seem to. Bad idea. Deaf and hard-of-hearing people want to know what’s going on, just like everyone else. And word will get around that you care about inclusion. We use our social media networks to ensure that. Give us your video and audio files and in a day or four they’ll all be accessible. And accurate to a T. Our editors are experienced professionals.
